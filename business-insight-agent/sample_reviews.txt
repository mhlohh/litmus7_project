The espresso taste is amazing and the temperature is always perfect!
The companion app crashes frequently on my Android device during connection setup.
Packaging was beautiful and it arrived a day earlier than expected.
I wish there was an option to schedule brewing via Alexa or voice commands, not just the mobile app.
It takes way too long to heat up in the morning compared to my old manual coffee machine.
The water reservoir is a bit small; I have to refill it after every two cups.
The self-cleaning feature works wonders and saves me a lot of time.
Customer support took 4 days to reply to my email about a leaking gasket.
The machine emitted a strong plastic smell during the first three uses, which was concerning.
Integrating it with smart home hubs like Google Home was extremely easy and took less than two minutes.
It would be great if they released a larger capacity version for office spaces or big families.
The price is a bit high, but the quality of the coffee definitely justifies the cost.
The milk frother stopped working after only two weeks of light usage.
The touch screen is very responsive and the user interface is intuitive.
Sometimes the coffee grinder gets clogged and it is quite difficult to clear it out.
